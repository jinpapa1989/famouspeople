10 Ghanaian Fashion Icons Changing the Game in 2025

By Admin | June 28, 2025

Ghana’s fashion scene continues to inspire the continent and the world. In 2025, these ten style trailblazers are not just following trends — they’re setting them.

  1. Nana Akua Addo – Slaying red carpets with bold couture choices and dramatic silhouettes.
  2. Serwaa Amihere – Mixing elegance with modern class in media appearances.
  3. Deborah Vanessa (Sister Derby) – A fearless blend of afro-futurism and playfulness.
  4. Okuntakinte – Pioneering genderless and expressive fashion.
  5. Afua Rida – Styling celebrities and global brands with a polished touch.
  6. Kwesi Arthur – A trendsetter for Gen Z streetwear lovers.
  7. Victoria Michaels – Commanding global runways and brand campaigns.
  8. Akosua Vee – Championing modest and powerful looks.
  9. Kojo Manuel – Bringing the heat to nightlife and fashion collabs.
  10. Elikem Kumordzie – Actor and designer pushing traditional menswear forward.

From red carpets to Instagram feeds, these icons are redefining what it means to be fashion-forward in Ghana and beyond.
